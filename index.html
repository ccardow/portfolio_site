My name is Cam Cardow

In my life, I've been a successful and internationally recognised editorial cartoonist, a web editor, art director and now a UI/UX product designer. I was the recipient of the national Newspaper Award in my second year of cartooning after being nominated in my first year. In total, I was honored six times in spite of taking time away from the field.
In 2013, I found myself to be a refugee from the diminished newspaper industry and took some time away to be a better father, as well as setting my sails for another life adventure. A friend suggested I try my hand at a local high tech firm and so I reentered the job market as a web designer.
As these things tend to happen, I discovered hidden talents in UI/UX design after being given the opportunity of creating app components for CORELine, a map-based spatial data solution for oil companies. And as things happen, after my mentor and colleague moved on, I took over the lead product design role.
My first kick at the can was PlanworX, a spatially enabled application, which became the flagship app at i-Open Technologies. I so surprised the CEO and myself with a tight, cohesive and intuitive design that soon after I found myself designing different iterations of PlanworX, such as WorXlog, as well as for the oil & gas and construction sectors
From there, I created CommandPost, Finpedient and finally Agri-Lyze, which the CEO, a former Silicon Valley VP of a Fortune 500 Company, remarked was the best-looking product demo he'd ever seen. High praise.
